5. Business Interpretation

Why KNN Won

  • The data appears to contain strong local similarity patterns among customers.
  • A larger K captured stable neighborhood structure better than smaller K values.
  • The very high recall suggests KNN is especially effective at catching true renewal cases.

Why Tree Still Matters

  • The tree explains which factors drive outcomes, which is critical for management communication.
  • It highlights actionable levers such as usage drop, tenure, NPS, and feature adoption.
  • Its rules can support customer success playbooks even if it is not the final top scorer.
Interpretive takeaway: If the main objective is pure predictive performance, KNN(k=29) is the preferred model. If the objective is managerial explainability and operational rule design, the Decision Tree remains highly useful as a companion model.

6. Executive Recommendation

Recommended Production Direction

  • Primary predictive model: KNN with K = 29
  • Primary explanation model: Classification Tree
  • Reason: KNN currently provides the best out-of-sample F1-score and recall, while the tree provides the clearest business interpretation.
  • Recommended operating model: Use KNN for scoring and use the tree for executive explanation, retention rule design, and intervention prioritization.
