8. Business Recommendations

Business Area Recommendation
Retention Campaigns Prioritize Cluster 0 for proactive retention outreach, usage recovery playbooks, and closer customer success management.
Targeted Marketing Design different messages by cluster. Cluster 0 should receive value-reinforcement and recovery messaging, while Cluster 1 should receive growth-oriented messaging.
Pricing Strategies Avoid one-size-fits-all pricing. Cluster 0 may need retention-focused commercial offers, while Cluster 1 is a better candidate for premium bundles or selective upsell.
Product Design Cluster 0 suggests a need to reduce disengagement. Product teams should investigate friction points related to usage decline and customer health deterioration.
Account Management Use Cluster 1 as the main base for upsell, premium offerings, and loyalty development because it shows healthier behavior and higher renewal.
Executive takeaway: The most important implication is that one segment shows a sharply lower renewal rate and much higher recent usage decline. That segment should be the immediate priority for retention and proactive intervention.

9. Final Conclusion

The K-Means analysis identified two customer segments. Cluster 0 represents a smaller but clearly riskier segment with much lower renewal, higher recent usage decline, and slightly weaker engagement and satisfaction. Cluster 1 represents the broader, healthier customer base with more stable usage and materially stronger renewal outcomes.

From a business perspective, this segmentation is actionable across marketing, pricing, product design, and retention planning. The strongest immediate value comes from using Cluster 0 as a retention-priority segment and Cluster 1 as an upsell and loyalty segment.

Report basis: K-Means clustering on 16 standardized customer behavior and operational variables, with optimal K selected by silhouette score.
